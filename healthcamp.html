Health Camp Services.

Aarogyasri is the flag ship scheme of the state government with an aim to provide quality health care to poor. The aim of the government is to achieve "HEALTH FOR ALL" in Telangana, and to bring awareness among the rural masses about the diseases they suffer from and to provide free medical advice and Medicines for the common ailments. Health Camps are main source of mobilizing the Beneficiaries. Trust is decided to conduct mega health camps at revenue division level. The important role played by the health camps included the following activities to be conducted by Network Hospitals.
